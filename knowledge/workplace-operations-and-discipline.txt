WORKPLACE OPERATIONS AND DISCIPLINE

EMPLOYEE TIME CLOCK

Employees may clock in up to five minutes before or after their scheduled shift. Lunch breaks are 45 minutes unpaid.

--------------------------------------------------

CELL PHONE POLICY

Personal phone use is prohibited during work hours. Phones must be stored in lockers or vehicles.

--------------------------------------------------

EMPLOYEE LOCKERS

Lockers are assigned by management. Employees are responsible for security and cleanliness.

--------------------------------------------------

SOCIAL MEDIA POLICY

Employees may use social media on personal time. Company-related posts require approval. Confidential information must not be shared.

--------------------------------------------------

EMPLOYEE REFERENCES

Only the Office Manager may provide employment references.

--------------------------------------------------

PROGRESSIVE DISCIPLINE

Step 1: Verbal warning  
Step 2: Written warning and Performance Improvement Plan  
Step 3: Two-day unpaid suspension  
Step 4: Termination  

--------------------------------------------------

TERMINATION AND FINAL PAYCHECK

Employees are at-will. Two weeks notice is requested. Final pay is issued on the regular payday.
