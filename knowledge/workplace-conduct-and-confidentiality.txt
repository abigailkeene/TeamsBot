WORKPLACE CONDUCT, HARASSMENT, AND CONFIDENTIALITY

ANTI-DISCRIMINATION AND ANTI-HARASSMENT POLICY

RETINA ASSOCIATES prohibits workplace or sexual harassment by anyone including staff, supervisors, vendors, customers, or third parties.

Harassment is conduct that creates an intimidating, hostile, or offensive work environment or interferes with work performance.

Sexual harassment includes unwelcome sexual advances, requests for sexual favors, verbal or physical conduct of a sexual nature, sexual jokes, comments, gestures, touching, or displaying suggestive materials.

--------------------------------------------------

HARASSMENT COMPLAINT PROCEDURE

1. If comfortable, address the individual directly.
2. Report the issue to management.
3. If unresolved, contact senior management.

All complaints are investigated confidentially. Retaliation is prohibited.

--------------------------------------------------

CONFIDENTIALITY AND HIPAA

Employees must:
- Access patient information only as needed for job duties
- Share minimum necessary information
- Secure PHI
- Use strong passwords
- Avoid discussing PHI in public
- Report HIPAA violations immediately

Violations may result in discipline or legal consequences.
