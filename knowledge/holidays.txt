Holidays:

The Company observes six paid holidays and three additional paid days off:

• New Year’s Day
• Memorial Day
• Apple Blossom (½ day)
• Independence Day
• Labor Day
• Thanksgiving
• Day After Thanksgiving
• Christmas Eve (½ day)
• Christmas Day

If a holiday falls on a weekend, the office will close on the nearest Friday or Monday at senior management’s discretion.

Employees must not be on unpaid leave the day before and after to receive holiday pay.
