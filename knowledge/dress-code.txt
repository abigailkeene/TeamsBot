Dress Code

At RETINA ASSOCIATES, all employees are representatives of the office and are expected to present themselves in professional attire.

Front Desk Associates:
• Business casual attire
• Open-toe shoes permitted (not in clinical areas)

Doctors:
• Blue scrubs
• Lab coats allowed for lab duties

Technicians:
• Black top, black bottom scrubs
• Closed-toe shoes

General Hygiene:
• Uniforms must be clean and professional
• Name tags must always be worn
• Jeans, leggings worn as pants, and T-shirts are not allowed

Employees wearing inappropriate attire may be sent home to change, and this time will be unpaid.
