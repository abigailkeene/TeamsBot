DrFirst - How to Download & Sync Medications to ICP

Purpose:
The purpose of this training is to review how to download and sync the patient’s systemic medications from DrFirst into ICP.

------------------------------------------------------------
Step-by-Step Instructions
------------------------------------------------------------

1. In the Sys Meds control, select the eRx button to open DrFirst.

2. Enter the patient’s pharmacy information by selecting:
   - "Please set the default pharmacy"
   OR
   - Bypass if the pharmacy is not known.

3. A list of medications should populate in the Active Medications section in the middle of the page.

   If medications do not populate or the patient reports additional medications:
   - Click on "Show Medication History"

4. Select the time period to search for Medication History from Other Sources using the drop-down menu.

5. Select any of the appropriate medications to add to the patient’s medication list by selecting the check box next to the desired medications.

   Then click:
   - "Add to Active Medications"

   Close the DrFirst window.

6. Once back in the Encounter window, click:
   - "Sync DrFirst" from the Sys Meds control.

   All Active Medications will be added to the patient’s chart.

------------------------------------------------------------
Summary
------------------------------------------------------------

This process ensures that the patient’s systemic medications are accurately downloaded from DrFirst and synchronized into the ICP chart, allowing providers to view and manage up-to-date medication records.
