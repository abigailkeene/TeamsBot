TIME OFF REQUESTS (PAYCHEX FLEX)

Q: How do I request time off?

A: To request time off through Paychex Flex:

1. Log in to Paychex Flex at https://www.paychexflex.com
2. On the dashboard, go to "Time Off" or "Time & Attendance"
3. Click "Request Time Off"
4. Choose the date range
5. Select the type of leave (PTO, sick, etc.)
6. Add notes if needed
7. Submit the request

You will receive a confirmation once the request is approved.

If you experience login issues or need help, contact an administrator or your manager.

IT SUPPORT REQUESTS

Q: Where can I submit an IT support request?

A: You have three options:

1. Click the Tech Team Solutions icon in your system tray (bottom-right corner of your screen).  
   This is the red triangle icon.

2. Use the “Create Ticket” button in the Knowledge Base portal.

3. Contact an administrator directly via Microsoft Teams or email if the issue is urgent or you cannot access the above tools.

REQUESTING ACCESS TO PLATFORMS

Q: How do I request access to an online platform or service required for my job?

A: If you need access to a platform such as Lynx, MDI, Optos, or AdvancedMD:

1. Contact an administrator via Microsoft Teams or email
2. Include the name of the platform
3. Specify your role and access level (view-only, full access, admin)
4. Mention any deadlines or urgency

This ensures your request is processed efficiently.

PHISHING AND SECURITY REPORTING

Q: How do I report a phishing email or suspicious activity?

A: If you receive a phishing email or notice suspicious activity:

1. Do not click any links or download attachments
2. Report the email immediately by:
   - Forwarding it to ehumphries@retinava.com
   - Forwarding it to support@techteamsolutions.com
   - Using the “Report Phishing” button in Outlook (if available)

If you clicked a link or entered information, contact IT immediately via Teams or phone.

Your quick response helps protect the entire organization.
