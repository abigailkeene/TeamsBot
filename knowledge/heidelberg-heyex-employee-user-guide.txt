Heidelberg Heyex Employee User Guide

Purpose

This guide provides Retina Associates clinical and imaging staff with instructions for using Heidelberg Heyex for ophthalmic imaging workflow management, image storage, and diagnostic image review.

Heyex is used to:
- Capture retinal images
- Store diagnostic imaging
- Review OCT and fundus photography
- Compare historical images
- Support physician clinical decisions
- Integrate with MDI and Optos systems

Heyex is a core imaging platform and must be used accurately and consistently.

What Is Heidelberg Heyex

Heidelberg Heyex is an ophthalmic imaging software platform used with Heidelberg imaging devices such as:
- OCT scanners
- Fundus cameras
- Angiography systems

It manages:
- Image acquisition
- Patient imaging records
- Historical image comparison
- Device integration
- Workflow coordination

Who Uses Heyex

Heyex is used by:
- Imaging technicians
- Clinical technicians
- Physicians
- Scribes
- Clinical support staff

Accessing Heyex

1. Log into the imaging workstation
2. Launch Heidelberg Heyex
3. Sign in with assigned credentials

Access is role-based and tracked.

Patient Search

1. Enter patient name, DOB, or MRN
2. Select patient from results
3. Open patient imaging record

Patient Verification

Verify patient identity before imaging:
- Full name
- Date of birth
- Medical record number

Never image the wrong patient.

Image Acquisition

1. Select imaging device
2. Select exam type
3. Position patient
4. Capture images
5. Review image quality
6. Save images to patient record

Image Types

Heyex supports:
- OCT scans
- Fundus photography
- Angiography
- Autofluorescence

Images are automatically stored in the patient record.

Image Review

Providers can:
- Review current images
- Compare historical images
- Analyze disease progression
- Document findings

Image Integration

Heyex integrates with:
- Nextech IntelleChart (MDI)
- Optos Advance
- AthenaHealth

Images are available within the patient chart.

Image Export

Images may be exported only for approved clinical purposes.

Exports must follow HIPAA policy.

Common Issues & Troubleshooting

Cannot Log In
- Verify credentials
- Restart workstation
- Contact IT

Device Not Connecting
- Verify cable connections
- Restart device
- Restart Heyex

Images Not Saving
- Check storage space
- Verify patient record
- Contact IT

Slow Performance
- Restart workstation
- Close unused applications
- Contact IT

Wrong Patient Selected
- Stop immediately
- Notify supervisor
- Document correction

Security & Patient Privacy

Heyex contains protected health information (PHI).

Staff must:
- Never share login credentials
- Never leave images open unattended
- Never export patient images without approval
- Always verify patient identity

Support

For Heyex issues:
- Document the issue
- Capture screenshots
- Identify device name
- Contact IT or imaging systems support
