ADDITIONAL LEAVE POLICIES

SEVERE WEATHER AND EMERGENCIES

Office closures are paid only if the employee was scheduled and did not call out prior to closure. Remote work may be required depending on role.

--------------------------------------------------

PARENTAL LEAVE

Eligible full-time employees with 12 months of service may take up to six weeks of unpaid parental leave for birth, adoption, or foster placement.

Healthcare benefits continue during leave. PTO does not accrue during unpaid leave.

--------------------------------------------------

VOTING

Employees are encouraged to vote. Any time off taken to vote is unpaid unless PTO is used.

--------------------------------------------------

RELIGIOUS ACCOMMODATIONS

RETINA ASSOCIATES provides reasonable accommodations for religious observance when possible. Requests should be submitted two weeks in advance.
