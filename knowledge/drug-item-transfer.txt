DRUG ITEM TRANSFER PROCEDURE
RETINA ASSOCIATES

--------------------------------------------------
PURPOSE

This document outlines the procedure for transferring drug inventory items between Retina Associates locations to ensure accurate tracking, accountability, and compliance.

--------------------------------------------------
SCOPE

This procedure applies to all clinical and administrative staff responsible for handling, transporting, and documenting drug inventory transfers between facilities.

--------------------------------------------------
RESPONSIBILITIES

Clinic Staff:
- Prepare drugs for transfer
- Verify quantities and expiration dates
- Ensure proper packaging and documentation

Receiving Clinic:
- Verify received items
- Confirm quantities and condition
- Update inventory records

Management:
- Approve transfers when required
- Ensure compliance with inventory policies

--------------------------------------------------
TRANSFER PROCEDURE

1. Initiating a Transfer
- Identify the drug item(s) needing transfer
- Verify item name, lot number, quantity, and expiration date
- Obtain approval from management if required

2. Preparing the Transfer
- Package drugs securely according to temperature and handling requirements
- Label package with:
  - Drug name
  - Quantity
  - Lot number
  - Expiration date
  - Sending and receiving location

3. Documentation
- Complete the Drug Item Transfer form
- Include:
  - Date of transfer
  - Sending clinic
  - Receiving clinic
  - Drug details
  - Quantity transferred
  - Staff signatures

4. Transport
- Use approved courier or internal transport method
- Maintain temperature control if required

5. Receiving Process
- Inspect package upon arrival
- Verify contents against transfer documentation
- Report discrepancies immediately

6. Inventory Update
- Update inventory system at both sending and receiving locations
- File completed transfer documentation

--------------------------------------------------
DISCREPANCIES OR DAMAGE

- Report immediately to management
- Do not use damaged or compromised drugs
- Follow incident reporting procedures

--------------------------------------------------
COMPLIANCE

All transfers must comply with:
- Internal inventory policies
- State and federal regulations
- Manufacturer handling requirements

Failure to follow this procedure may result in disciplinary action.

--------------------------------------------------
DOCUMENT CONTROL

This procedure is reviewed periodically and updated as needed by management.
