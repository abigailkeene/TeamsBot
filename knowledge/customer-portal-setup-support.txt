Customer Portal Setup Support Document

------------------------------------------------------------
Signing Up for CustomerCentral
------------------------------------------------------------

1. Navigate to:
   www.optos.com

2. Click on “Customers”.

------------------------------------------------------------
Create an Account
------------------------------------------------------------

1. Select “Register Now”.

2. Complete the registration form.

3. Your account will be auto-activated if:
   - Your Site Number matches
   - Your Zip Code matches

4. If there is a discrepancy, activation will occur within 24–48 hours.

------------------------------------------------------------
Password Setup
------------------------------------------------------------

After registration, you will receive an email prompting you to reset your password.

Password requirements:
- 8–20 characters
- Upper and lower case letters
- At least one special character (example: * or #)

Follow the instructions in the email to create your password.

------------------------------------------------------------
Resetting Your Password
------------------------------------------------------------

1. Click “Forgot Password?” on the login page.

2. You will receive an email with reset instructions.

3. Follow the instructions to reset your password.

Password requirements:
- 8–20 characters
- Upper and lower case letters
- At least one special character (example: * or #)

------------------------------------------------------------
Sign In
------------------------------------------------------------

1. Enter your email address.
2. Enter your password.
3. Select Sign In.

------------------------------------------------------------
CustomerCentral Home Page
------------------------------------------------------------

After signing in, you will be directed to the CustomerCentral home page.

------------------------------------------------------------
Ordering Marketing Materials
------------------------------------------------------------

1. Click on:
   - “Patient Marketing”
   - “Order Your Marketing Materials”

2. Customers under support or warranty receive materials free of charge.

3. Customers without a service contract may order materials for a minimal charge at:
   www.curryprinting.com/optos

4. Custom marketing materials are available at:
   www.curryprinting.com/optospartner

------------------------------------------------------------
Placing an Order
------------------------------------------------------------

1. Select items and add them to your cart.

2. Go to the cart and select “Proceed to Checkout”.

3. Select your shipping address or add multiple shipping addresses.

4. Scroll down and select “Place Order”.

------------------------------------------------------------
Order Confirmation
------------------------------------------------------------

After placing an order:
- You will see a confirmation screen
- You will receive a confirmation email

------------------------------------------------------------
Manuals and Technical Support
------------------------------------------------------------

CustomerCentral provides access to:
- Product manuals
- Technical support documentation

------------------------------------------------------------
Training Materials
------------------------------------------------------------

Training materials are available for providers and staff to:
