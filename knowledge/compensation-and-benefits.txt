COMPENSATION AND BENEFITS

COMPENSATION REVIEWS

Annual performance evaluations support growth and merit-based compensation increases.

--------------------------------------------------

PAY PERIODS

Employees are paid bi-weekly via direct deposit. If payday falls on a holiday, payment is made the preceding business day.

--------------------------------------------------

BENEFITS

Full-time employees working 32 or more hours per week are eligible for medical insurance beginning the first of the month following hire.

--------------------------------------------------

EDUCATION REIMBURSEMENT

Employees may be reimbursed up to $2000 per year for accredited continuing education.

Procedure:
1. Submit course details for approval
2. Complete reimbursement request
3. Retain form
4. Submit receipts and proof of completion
