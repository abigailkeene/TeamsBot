Established Comprehensive Steps Checklist

Open summary - Note OCT/any other order and VA on encounter form
Open Chart, create Follow Up visit under "New"
If anyone has pre-populated, change tech initials to yours
Click HPI:
- Mark Covid
- Mental Status
- Prefill CC
- Note if Diabetic
- Check 2 boxes in Review of Systems
Order Images
Call Patient
Verify DOB
Ask about vision changes, flashes or floaters
Review AIC and last Fasting Blood Sugar if Diabetic
Review Medical History
Check Systematic Medications Reviewed and PSFH/ROS
Proceed to screening:
- Visual Acuity
- Confrontation Visual Fields
- Motility
- Pupils
- Check Pressures (IOP)
- Dilate
- Note Drops
- Click V&P Box
Take to Imaging


New Patient Comprehensive Steps Checklist

Open summary and locate referral
Open Chart
Create New Referral visit
Pre-document referral information
Click HPI:
- Covid
- Mental Status
- Prefill CC
- Note if Diabetic
- Review Systems
Order Images
Call Patient
Verify DOB
Review reason for visit
Review AIC and FBS if Diabetic
Collect Medical History
Review medications, surgeries, allergies
Review family and social history
Proceed to screening:
- Visual Acuity
- Confrontation Visual Fields
- Motility
- Pupils
- Check Pressures (IOP)
- Dilate
- Note Drops
- Click V&P Box
Take to Imaging


Post-Op Appointments

1 Day Post-Op:
- Bring pouch from tech station
- Chart: Tech initials, Covid, Mental Status, CC, 2 green boxes
- Ask about sleep and pain
- Remove bandage
- Check VA and IOP in surgical eye
- Confrontation, Motility, Pupils
- Administer first PO drops
- Review take-home bag
- No imaging

Every Other Post-Op Visit:
- Chart: Tech initials, Covid, Mental Status, CC, 2 green boxes
- Review sleeping and drops
- Check VA and IOP both eyes
- Dilate surgical eye only
- Order imaging


Laser Screening Steps

Open summary
Note laser type, location, and OCT
Open Chart, create Clinical Procedure Only visit
Add laser type, location, date, and count
Click HPI:
- Covid
- Mental Status
- Prefill CC
- Order images if needed
Create Consent
Verify DOB
Verify laser and location
Sign Consent
Proceed to screening:
- Visual Acuity OU
- Pressures OU
- Dilate laser eye
- Mark Drops
- Tetracaine first drop
- V&P check
Send to Imaging or Laser Room
