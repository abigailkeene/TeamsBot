GoTo Phone System Employee User Guide

Purpose

This guide provides Retina Associates staff with instructions for using the GoTo phone system for patient communication, internal coordination, voicemail management, and call handling.

The GoTo phone system is used for:
- Patient phone calls
- Appointment scheduling
- Prescription coordination
- Inter-office communication
- Call routing and voicemail
- After-hours coverage

The phone system is a critical part of clinic operations and patient care.

What Is GoTo

GoTo is a cloud-based VoIP phone system that provides:
- Desk phones
- Softphones (computer and mobile apps)
- Call queues
- Voicemail
- Call forwarding
- Auto-attendants

Calls are routed through the internet instead of traditional phone lines.

Who Uses GoTo

GoTo is used by:
- Front desk staff
- Scheduling teams
- Billing departments
- Clinical coordinators
- Physicians
- Management

Accessing GoTo

GoTo can be accessed through:
- Desk phone
- GoTo desktop application
- GoTo mobile app
- Web portal

Users log in with their assigned credentials.

Desk Phone Basics

Answering a Call
- Lift handset or press Answer

Ending a Call
- Hang up handset or press End

Placing a Call
- Dial number directly
- Press Call

Hold
- Press Hold button

Transfer
- Press Transfer
- Dial extension or number
- Complete transfer

Voicemail

Checking Voicemail
- Press Voicemail button
- Enter PIN if prompted

Voicemail may also be delivered to email.

Leaving Voicemail
- Call extension
- Wait for greeting
- Leave message

Call Queues

Call queues route incoming patient calls to available staff.

Examples:
- Front desk queue
- Scheduling queue
- Billing queue

Calls are distributed automatically.

Logging Into a Queue

1. Log into GoTo app or desk phone
2. Join assigned queue
3. Set status to Available

Call Forwarding

Call forwarding is used for:
- Remote work
- After-hours coverage
- Provider call coverage

Forwarding can be set:
- From desk phone
- From GoTo app
- From web portal

Auto-Attendant

The auto-attendant answers incoming calls and routes patients to:
- Front desk
- Scheduling
- Billing
- Clinical staff
- On-call provider

Call Recording

Some lines may be recorded for:
- Training
- Quality assurance
- Compliance

Call recording follows internal policy.

Common Issues & Troubleshooting

No Dial Tone
- Check network connection
- Reboot phone
- Verify ethernet cable

Calls Dropping
- Check internet connection
- Restart phone
- Contact IT

Cannot Log In
- Verify username and password
- Reset password if needed
- Contact IT

Voicemail Not Working
- Verify mailbox setup
- Check email integration
- Contact IT

Phone Not Ringing
- Check Do Not Disturb
- Check queue login status
- Verify volume settings

Remote Access

GoTo can be used remotely through:
- GoTo desktop app
- GoTo mobile app

Staff must use approved devices only.

Security & Patient Privacy

GoTo may contain patient information.

Staff must:
- Never record PHI outside approved systems
- Never share voicemail access codes
- Never forward patient calls to personal numbers without approval

Support

For GoTo issues:
- Document the issue
- Capture error messages
- Identify phone extension
- Contact IT support
