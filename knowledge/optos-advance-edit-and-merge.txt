Patient Imaged Under the Wrong Name

If the wrong patient name is selected on the tablet and the imaging session needs to be moved to the correct patient, follow the steps below.


SECTION 1: Edit Patient Study

Login to OptosAdvance  
(User must be granted privilege to Edit Patient Studies)

1. In the Patient Directory, search for the correct patient name.
2. Copy the correct patient ID number.
3. Locate the study that was done today under the wrong patient name.
4. Right-click on that patient and select:
   Edit Patient/Study
5. Delete the ID number currently shown.
6. Paste the correct patient ID number.
7. Make sure ALL options at the bottom are UNCHECKED.
8. Click Save.
9. When the warning screen appears, click Continue.


SECTION 2: Merge Patients

10. From the Patient Study Directory, search by Patient ID.
11. You will now see:
    - The correct patient record
    - The edited record with the wrong name

12. Check both boxes to the left of the two patient names.
13. Right-click on one of the names and select:
    Merge Patients

14. On the Merge Patients screen:
    - Uncheck the box that says:
      "Apply changes to all studies for the same patient"

15. If the patient on the LEFT is the correct record, select:
    Merge to Left

16. If the patient on the RIGHT is the correct record, select:
    Merge to Right

17. Click Continue at the warning screen.

18. The patient information in the study will now be updated and merged correctly.


End of Procedure
